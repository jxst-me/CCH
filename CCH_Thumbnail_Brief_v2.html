Golden Rules

Always
One template, every video

Navy gradient, brand badge top-right, text bottom-left, hosts bottom-right. No exceptions.

Always
Photo must breathe in the top half

The aerial or property shot should be clearly visible. Never let overlays kill the photo.

Never
Don't reuse the same host pose

Poses do communication work. Match emotion to content every time.

Never
No "Watch Now" buttons or extra badges

YouTube provides the play button. Extra UI elements create clutter.

Text Rules

Format
Line 1: Hook — 2–3 words

Most compelling stat, price, or surprise from the video. All caps.

Format
Line 2: Category — 1–2 words (largest)

Property type or topic. All caps. Must be the biggest text on the thumbnail.

Limit
3–4 words total maximum

If it won't fit on a thumbnail at 120px, it's too long.

Limit
No location in text unless it's the hook

The brand badge handles location passively. Exception: specific neighborhood names as a differentiator (e.g. CAROLINA FOREST vs BEACH CONDO).

2

Accent Color Rules

White — Default

All text is white with black outline. Used on every thumbnail, always.

Red — Warning / Negative

Use sparingly for key words in warning or "wrong choice" videos. e.g. the word "WRONG" or "CRASH" in red italic.

Gold — Premium / Luxury

Reserved for high-end listings or investment-focused content. Use on one word only as an accent.

Green — Good News / Positive

For "buyers win" or positive market news. One word accent only.
